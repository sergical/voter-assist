## Surveys

We survey voters, election officials, candidates, and other stakeholders to gather feedback for process improvement and voter experience enhancement.

These surveys, conducted by Elections Ontario, Léger, and Advanis from January 30 to March 14, include:

**Voters**

* Evaluate registration, voting location access, and ballot casting experiences
* Assess Elections Ontario advertising campaigns
* Measure public trust in Ontario's electoral process

**Local Election Office Staff and Election Officials**

* Feedback on advance voting and election day experiences
* Inform training, support, and operational process enhancements

**Candidates and Political Entities**

* Input on services, tools, and support received during the election

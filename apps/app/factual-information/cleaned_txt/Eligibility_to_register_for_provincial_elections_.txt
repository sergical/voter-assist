## Eligibility to Register for Provincial Elections

**A provincial general election has been called. [Election day](https://voterinformationservice.elections.on.ca/en/election/search?mode=postalCode) is February 27.**

**Update your information on the [Register](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/home) by February 17 or when you go to vote.**

To be eligible to register for an Ontario provincial election, you must be:

- 18 years of age or older
- a Canadian citizen
- a resident of Ontario

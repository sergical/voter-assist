# Political Financing

The [*Election Finances Act*](https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/90e07 "Opens in a new tab") requires the chief financial officer (CFO) of registered political entities to file financial statements annually, as well as after by-elections, general elections, and leadership contests.

You can find financial statements and publicly disclosable contribution information for registered:

- Political parties
- Constituency associations
- Candidates
- Leadership contestants
- Third-party advertisers

Use our [**Political Financing and Party Information**](http://finances.elections.on.ca/ "Opens in a new tab") application to search, browse, and download information.

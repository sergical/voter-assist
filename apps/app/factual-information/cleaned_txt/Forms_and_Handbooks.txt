# Forms and Handbooks {#forms-and-handbooks}

Find the financial reporting forms and documents, as required by the [*Election Finances
Act*](https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/90e07 "Opens in a new tab"){target="_blank"
rel="noopener noreferrer"}, for the different types of political
entities in Ontario below.

If you have any questions or require any additional information, you
can [contact
us](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/contact-us/contact-information-by-department.html#accordioncontactef "Opens in the same tab").

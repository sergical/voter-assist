# Media

**For in-person access to Elections Ontario's public scrutiny room,
please contact our [compliance department](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/contact-us/contact-information-by-department.html#accordioncontactef) by [phone](tel:18665669066) or
by [email](mailto:electfin@elections.on.ca).**

Important election dates and more can be found in our media releases.
Assets are available for media use.

We have outlined [rules for media](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/media-centre/media-rules-during-an-election.html) to
follow when reporting on provincial elections in Ontario.

If you would like to request other information or materials, please
contact [our media relations team](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/contact-us/contact-information-by-department.html#accordionmediacontact).

- [Third parties](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/political-entities-in-ontario/third-parties.html)
- [Information for Broadcasters and Publishers](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/media-centre/information-for-broadcasters-and-publishers.html)

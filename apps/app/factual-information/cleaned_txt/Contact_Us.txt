**Complaints about a violation of the *Election Act* or *Election Finances Act***:**

Anyone wishing to file a complaint must do so formally in writing by mail, fax or email to the Compliance Division, and include their full contact information.

Please provide a detailed description of your complaint, including the details of any other person who may be able to provide further information, and any documents that support your complaint.

Elections Ontario applies its Complaints, Investigation, and Enforcement Policy to all complaints.

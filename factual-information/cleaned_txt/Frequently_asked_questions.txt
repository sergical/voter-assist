## Where do I vote? 

If you are [eligible to
vote](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html){target="_self"
rel="noopener noreferrer"} in a provincial election, you can vote:

-   at your [local election
    office](https://elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionlocalelectionoffice);
    or
-   during [advance
    voting](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionadvance "Opens in the same tab");
    or
-   at the voting location on [election
    day](https://elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionelectionday);
    or
-   by [mail](https://elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionvbmEN);
    or
-   by [home
    visit](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionhomevisit)
    or [hospital
    visit](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/voting-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionhospital)

You can use our [Voter Information Service[Opens in a new
tab]{.sr-only}](https://voterinformationservice.elections.on.ca/en/election/search?mode=postalCode){target="_blank"
rel="noopener noreferrer"} to find your voting locations.

## Which ID do I need to vote in a provincial election? 

If you are on the Register, you need to present **one piece of ID**
**showing your name** to vote. If you are **not** on the Register, you
must present **one piece of ID showing both your name and home address**
to vote.

Find examples of [accepted ID to
vote](https://elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/id-to-vote-in-provincial-elections.html#accordionIDtovote){target="_self"
rel="noopener noreferrer"}.

rel="noopener noreferrer"}.

## I need assistance with voting 

We strive to make voting accessible to all voters. Services for
accessible voting include assistive voting technology, assistance at
voting locations, and voting by mail or home visit.

Find more information about [accessible
voting](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/accessible-voting.html).

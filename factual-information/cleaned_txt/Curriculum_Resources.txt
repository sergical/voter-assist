# Curriculum Resources

Looking to find information about provincial elections in Ontario? Here are some lessons on key themes, processes and systems that define Ontario's provincial elections. These resources have been created to make learning about Ontario's provincial elections easy. Access them below or request hard copies by completing the [resource order form](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/resource-centre/learning-about-elections/resource-order-form.html "Opens in the same tab").

# By-election Maps

**Types of Maps Available:**

- **Atlas Map **-- 8.5 x 11 broad overview maps of each electoral district, including major geographical features.
- **Overview Detailed Wall Map** -- 34 x 44 detailed wall map of an electoral district.
- **Polling Division Wall Map** -- 34 x 44 detailed wall map(s) of all polling division boundaries including inset maps of cities and towns for an electoral district.

You can order maps using our [Map Order Form](https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/map-order-form.html "Opens in the same tab").
